About Us

Wellness Meets Beauty

At Drip MedSpa, we believe that beauty and wellness are inseparable. Founded with a vision to create New Jersey's first boutique medi spa, we combine medical expertise with luxurious care to help every client look and feel their absolute best. Our approach is intentional, personalized, and results-driven.

Nena Kallopoulos

Nena Kallopoulos is a Board Certified Physician Associate with over 15 years of clinical experience spanning Emergency Medicine, Pediatrics, and Wellness. Her diverse medical background gives her a unique perspective on aesthetics -- understanding not just how to enhance beauty, but how to do so safely and in harmony with each patient's overall health.

Driven by her passion for helping people feel confident in their own skin, Nena founded Drip MedSpa as New Jersey's first boutique medi spa. Her vision was to create a space where clinical precision meets luxury, where every treatment is thoughtfully tailored, and where clients are treated like family from the moment they walk through the door.

Under Nena's leadership, Drip MedSpa has become a destination for those seeking expert-level cosmetic and wellness treatments in an atmosphere that feels anything but clinical. She continues to train, innovate, and lead her team of talented providers -- the Drip Dolls -- with the same dedication and care that built the practice from day one.
