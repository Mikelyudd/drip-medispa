The Drip Process

Our state-of-the-art laser hair removal uses advanced diode technology to target hair follicles at the root, delivering safe, effective, and long-lasting results for all skin types. Here is what to expect when you choose DR!P for your laser hair removal journey.

1

Prep

Shave the treatment area 24 hours before your appointment. Avoid sun exposure for at least two weeks prior, and refrain from waxing, plucking, or threading as the laser needs the hair root intact to be effective.

2

Your Appointment

Your provider will apply a cooling gel and use the laser to target hair follicles with precise, controlled pulses of light. Most sessions take between 15 and 60 minutes depending on the treatment area. A built-in cooling system minimizes discomfort throughout.

3

Aftercare

Avoid direct sun exposure and apply SPF 30+ daily to treated areas. You may experience mild redness that subsides within a few hours. Expect treated hairs to shed naturally over the next 1 to 3 weeks as the follicles release.

Staying Smooth

Most clients see significant hair reduction after 6 sessions spaced 4 to 6 weeks apart. Periodic maintenance treatments may be needed once or twice a year to keep your results looking flawless. Consistency is the key to silky, smooth skin year-round.
