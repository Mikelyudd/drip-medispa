How It Works

The Drip Process

Our wrinkle relaxer treatments use neuromodulators like Botox, Dysport, and Xeomin to temporarily relax the muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles. Our expert injectors assess your facial structure and movement patterns to deliver results that look natural, refreshed, and uniquely you.

1

Prep

Your treatment begins with a thorough consultation. We discuss your goals, assess your facial anatomy, and create a customized plan. Avoid blood thinners and alcohol 24 hours before your appointment for optimal results.

2

Your Appointment

The treatment itself takes only 10-15 minutes. Using ultra-fine needles, your injector precisely places the neuromodulator in targeted muscles. Most patients describe the sensation as a slight pinch with minimal discomfort.

3

Aftercare

Stay upright for 4 hours post-treatment and avoid rubbing or massaging the treated areas. Skip intense exercise for 24 hours. Results gradually appear within 3-7 days and reach full effect by two weeks.

What To Know

Wrinkle Relaxer Tips

Most patients schedule their follow-up treatments every 3-4 months, though this can vary based on your metabolism and the areas treated. Over time, with consistent treatments, many patients find they can extend the interval between appointments as the muscles become trained to relax. We recommend booking your next appointment before you leave to ensure optimal timing.

Botox, Dysport, and Xeomin are all FDA-approved neuromodulators that work similarly but have subtle differences. Botox is the most well-known and versatile option. Dysport tends to spread more, making it ideal for larger areas like the forehead. Xeomin is a "naked" neurotoxin without additives, which may be beneficial for those who have developed resistance to other products. Your injector will recommend the best option during your consultation.

Wrinkle relaxers aren't just for the upper face. Lower face treatments can address chin dimpling, a gummy smile, downturned mouth corners (DAO), platysmal bands in the neck, and masseter reduction for jawline slimming and TMJ relief. These advanced techniques require a skilled injector who understands the complex anatomy of the lower face to deliver safe, beautiful results.
